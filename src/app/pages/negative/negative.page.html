Prevention Keep yourself and the ones you love safe. Disease Prevention To protect yourself from contracting HIV:
  • Abstain from having multiple sex partners.
  • Use condoms during sex.
  • Do not share cutting tools; nailcutters, razors, needles.

To protect yourself from contracting Chlamydia/Gonorrhea:
  • Use condoms. Use a male latex condom or a female polyurethine condom during sexual contact.
  • Limit your numbers of sex partners; having multiple sex partners puts you at a high risk.
  • Test yourself regularly.
  • Refrain from oral or anal sex.

To protect yourself from contracting Hepatitis B:
  • Get the Hepatitis B vaccine.
  • Use condoms during sex.
  • Avoid sharing of needles and other cutting tools.

To protect yourself from contracting Malaria:
  • Use a treated mosquito net.
  • Pour off and clear any water that may stagnate.
  • Clear bushes near the homestead.
  • Make use of mosquito repellants.
